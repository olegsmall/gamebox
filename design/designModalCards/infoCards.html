Welcome to Gamebox!

General info

This is a platform for sharing games between players. Our members can sell, buy, rent the games and comment / rate these games and other members.

Join us

Being a member of our great platform means to have access to the most interesting games of various genres and play in real time.

Payment

Secure payment can be made in cash, by check or Paypal. Choose your game and go ahead!

How it works

You must register or log in to be able to sell, buy or rent games and be our member. As a member, you can also write the comments or rate the games and other members, post articles or tips and show certain statistics. In addition, our member can communicate with other members through our chat system, complain to the site administrator about some members and be blocked by the site administrator. And finally, the site administrator can give our member the right to be the site administrator.
